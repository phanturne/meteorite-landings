Meteorite Landings

The purpose of this visualization is to analyze 34,513 meteorite landings from the past 150 years and identify patterns in their occurrences. Meteorites are solid pieces of debris from objects that originate in outer space and have passed through the atmosphere. Our data visualization helps display concentrations of meteorite landings over time.

This project is designed for: “CSE: Data Programming for Visualization, Fall 2022”
